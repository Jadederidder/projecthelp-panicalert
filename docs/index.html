Activation walkthrough

Nine simple steps from download to protected.

Have your activation SMS or email open before you start — you'll need the coupon code inside it.

  1. 1

    Download the app

    Use the link in your activation email, or search "Bidvest Panic Alert" in the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

  2. 2

    Open the app and tap Log in

    Launch Bidvest Panic Alert on your phone and tap the orange Log in button to begin.

  3. 3

    Allow location access

    Give the app permission to use your device location. This is critical — without it, the response team can't be sent to you.

  4. 4

    Enter your cell number

    Use the same number you gave when you bought the policy. Accept the terms and conditions, then tap Submit.

  5. 5

    Enter the SMS verification code

    An OTP arrives by SMS within seconds. Type it in and tap Submit. Didn't get it? Tap "Did not get the code?" to resend.

  6. 6

    Complete your profile

    Fill in your name, surname, email and date of birth. The response team uses these details when you panic, so accuracy matters.

  7. 7

    Activate your subscription

    Select Coupon Code and enter the code from your activation SMS or email, then tap Activate Subscription. Dependants follow the same steps with their own coupon code.

  8. 8

    Run a Test Panic

    Press and hold the Test Panic button until it turns blue. You'll get a confirmation call from the control room within seconds — this proves your setup is working.

  9. ✓

    You're protected

    Your Panic Alert app is now active and ready to use, 24 hours a day, anywhere in South Africa.
